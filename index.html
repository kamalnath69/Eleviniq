Our Features

Onsite Classes for Colleges & Schools

Edvatiq partners with colleges snd schools to offer online and offline training sessions tailored to meet the needs of students. Whether in-person or virtual, our programs are customized to align with institutional requirements and provide impactful learning experiences.

Hands-On Learning with Assessments

Our platform ensures students gain practical experience through hands-on assignments, quizzes, and real-world projects. With structured assessments, learners can track their progress, improve their skills, and earn certifications that validate their expertise.

Expert-Curated Courses

Learn from industry experts who provide real-world knowledge and insights. Our courses are designed to equip you with both foundational skills and advanced techniques in high-demand fields like AI, Cloud Computing, Full Stack Development, UI/UX Designing, and Data Science.

Real-World Application

At Edvatiq, we believe in bridging the gap between theory and practice. Our courses are designed to help you tackle real-world challenges, using tools and techniques that are widely used in the industry. You'll work on projects that simulate actual work scenarios, allowing you to develop problem-solving skills and experience that can be directly applied in your career. Whether it’s building a website, creating a data model, or designing a user interface, our focus is on making sure you gain the skills and confidence to succeed in any professional setting.

Why Edvatiq for Institutional Training?

Interactive Live Training

Our live classes, led by industry experts, offer interactive learning with real-time discussions and insights. Students can collaborate with peers and engage in hands-on learning experiences. This approach ensures a dynamic and relevant education.

Customizable Curriculum

The training modules are tailored to match the institution's academic calendar and student learning pace, ensuring maximum understanding and retention.

Progress Tracking and Feedback

Students can track their learning progress through regular assessments and receive personalized feedback to improve their skills effectively.

Post-Course Career Assistance

Our career support team provides guidance, including resume building, interview preparation, and networking opportunities, ensuring a smooth transition from learning to a successful career.

Flexible Training Mode

Institutions can choose between online or offline training sessions based on their specific requirements. Whether it's remote learning or in-person classes, we adapt to your needs.

Seamless Integration with LMS

Edvatiq courses integrate seamlessly into your LMS, enabling smooth course delivery and progress tracking. It won’t disrupt students’ academic schedules. The integration adds value without extra burden.
